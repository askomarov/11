Overview

The pm2.5 charcoal filters used in PROORB make sure that not only are solid and semi-solid particles filtered off, but also smoke, ash, bad-odour and soot are thoroughly removed from the air that the wearer is inhaling.

This makes the air 99.97% clean for the wearer to breathe. Contemporarily, all the visors in the market have fixed filters, however, PROORB has modular filters containing discs. The company offers PROORB with HEPA and charcoal filters, but due to their modular nature, the wearer can put any filter of their choice in these discs depending on individual preferences.
